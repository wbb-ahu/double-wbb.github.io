Biography

I received the Ph.D. degree in computer science from [Anhui University] of China in 2024. I am currently a postdoctor a Postdoctor in [School of Computer Science and Technology] at [Anhui University].

My current research interests include graph data representation and learning. I have published 10 papers in top journal, such as IEEE T-PAMI, IEEE T-NNLS, Pattern Recognition. I am a reviewer for a number of journals, such as the IEEE TRANSACTIONS ON Neural Networks and Learning Systerm, Parttern Recognition, IEEE Sensors Journal.

汪倍倍，于2024年获得安徽大学计算机科学与技术学院博士学位，导师为江波教授、丁宏强教授。现为安徽大学计算机科学与技术学院博士后，主要从事结构模式识别、图数据的表示与学习等方向的研究。在国内外高水平期刊发表论文 10 余篇，其中以第一/导师第一、本人第二在人工智能顶级期刊IEEE T-PAMI发表论文3篇。目前担任IEEE T-NNLS, PR等期刊的审稿人。
